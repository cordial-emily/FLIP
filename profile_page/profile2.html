A Little about Me

I enjoy watercolor, vibrant colors and depicting flowers in an innovative unique way that allows for the perfection and the messiness of nature to coincide together on the paper. I love to translate close-ups of flowers onto paper. I'm attracted to brilliantly colored floral abstractions, flowers that warm the soul and present the viewer with a personality all their own.
