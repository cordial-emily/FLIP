A Little about Me

I'm a psychotherapist in private practice in Sacramento with over thirty years experience working with individuals, couples and families. I'm also an author of Making Peace With Your Adult Children: A Guide to Family Healing (HarperCollins) and numerous articles, stories and poems. My memoir, Missing Father, will be published soon.
