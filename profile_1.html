Artist Sacramento

Shauna L. Smith

She is a psychotherapist in private practice in Sacramento with over thirty years experience working with individuals, couples and families. She is the author of Making Peace With Your Adult Children: A Guide to Family Healing (HarperCollins) and numerous articles, stories and poems. Her memoir, Missing Father, will be published soon.
